Who we are

Since 2015, our founder—a veterinarian and lifelong animal lover—has brought a farm-stead tradition of hands-on care from our family’s small farm overseas to Hollywood Grooming. Back home, we practiced veterinary medicine and tended to every creature, big and small.

Today, that same compassion guides every bath, haircut, and nail trim. We treat your pet like one of our own—gentle, attentive, and always ready with a wagging tail (or purring cuddle) in mind.

Frequently Asked Questions

  1. Please arrive with your pet on a leash or in a secured carrier
  2. Brush out any mats or heavy tangles before you come (an extra dematting charge may apply for severe mats)
  3. Make sure your pet has gone to the bathroom within an hour of their appointment
  4. Let us know about any health issues (allergies, arthritis, recent surgeries) so we can adapt our handling

Most breeds benefit from a professional grooming every 4–6 weeks to keep coat, skin, nails, and ears healthy.

  1. Long-haired or double-coated breeds (e.g. Shih Tzu, Pomeranian) often need a full groom every 4 weeks, with a mid-cycle mini groom or luxury bath for a refresh
  2. Short-haired breeds (e.g. Beagle, Boxer) can go 6–8 weeks between full grooms, with a mid-cycle mini groom or luxury bath for a refresh
